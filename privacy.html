Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Athena Capital Partners LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at athenacapitalnv.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Service interest / subject
  • Message content

This information is submitted through FormSubmit.co, a third-party form processing service, and delivered to us via email.

Information Collected Automatically

With your consent, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • Browser type and device information
  • Approximate geographic location (city/region level)
  • Scroll depth and outbound link clicks

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests for quotes
  • Understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it
  • Ensure our website functions properly

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies only when you consent to analytics tracking. You can manage your preference at any time by clicking the cookie settings link in our website footer. The cookies we use are:

  • _ga — Google Analytics cookie used to distinguish users (expires after 2 years)
  • _ga_<ID> — Google Analytics cookie used to maintain session state (expires after 2 years)

If you decline analytics, no tracking cookies are set.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — website usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • FormSubmit.co — contact form processing (FormSubmit.co)
  • Google Fonts — web font delivery (Google Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting (GitHub Privacy Statement)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information as necessary with the third-party services listed above to operate our website and respond to your inquiries.

7. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email system for as long as necessary to fulfill your request. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of analytics tracking via the cookie consent banner or footer link
  • Request access to any personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@athenacapitalnv.com.

9. Security

Our website is served over HTTPS and includes security headers (Content Security Policy, Referrer Policy, X-Content-Type-Options) to protect against common web vulnerabilities. While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
