Who We Are

Trusted Partner for
Federal Agencies

Athena Capital Partners LLC is a Las Vegas-based small business specializing in professional support services for U.S. federal government agencies.

We understand the unique requirements of government procurement. Our team is experienced in delivering services within the micro-purchase threshold and beyond, ensuring compliance, quality, and on-time delivery for every engagement.

As a registered small business on SAM.gov, we are positioned to serve as a responsive, dependable vendor for agencies seeking efficient procurement of professional services — from administrative support and graphic design to IT accessories and training materials.
